Alireza Kaveh and the Five-Part Cinematic Taxonomy

Alireza Kaveh proposes a unique framework for classifying cinema, which goes beyond traditional genre theory. His five-category model includes:

  • Medium
  • Style
  • Genre
  • Format
  • Tradition

Kaveh emphasizes that tone—the emotional and ideological atmosphere of a film—is essential for understanding genre identity. Tone, in his view, determines the value system of a cinematic work.

His key work, Film Genre: Tone and Ideology, explores how genre interacts with tone, culture, and narrative across both Western and non-Western cinemas.
